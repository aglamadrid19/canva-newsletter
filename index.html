What is the golden ratio?

The Golden Ratio is one secret every designer has used in their career. Put simply, it’s a complicated math formula that’s hidden from plain sight.

Designers have long since harnessed The Golden Ratio to create organic, compositions that are naturally pleasing to the eye - after all, we see it in nature from sunflowers to seashells.

Good news: Luckily for you, there’s no need to remember how to use this mathematic equation in your designs. Canva has thousands of templates that already incorporate The Golden Ratio, so you’re all set to design like a true designer.
